Person 2 — Feature Engineering

8 features extracted per transaction

  • Log-normalised amount — compresses heavy-tailed XRP distribution
  • Z-score vs baseline — how far this amount is from the historical mean
  • Address velocity — transactions per address per minute
  • Transaction type encoding — Payment, Offer, Escrow, NFT, AMM…
  • Time-of-day (sin/cos) — cyclic encoding of ledger close time
  • Destination frequency — how often this address appears as a receiver
  • Amount percentile rank — position in the historical distribution
  • Asset pair — DEX trading pair for offer transactions

Isolation Forest

Unsupervised anomaly scorer — no labelled data needed. Scores 0→1, threshold at 0.65.

Isolates anomalies by building random trees. Outliers require fewer splits — so they get higher anomaly scores automatically.
